5-Ingredient Salmon Recipe

Description

This easy salmon recipe is a delicious way to prepare fresh fillets using just a few ingredients from your pantry.

Serving Size and Prep Time

Prep Time: 5 mins

Cook Time 10 minutes

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 tblsp. garlic powder
  • 1 tblsp. dried basil
  • .5 tsp. salt
  • 4 (6 oz.) salmon fillets
  • 2 tblsp. butter
  • 4 lemon wedges

Steps

  1. Stir garlic poweder, basil, and salt together in a small bowl. Rub the mixture evenly over salmon.
  2. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add salmon and cook until browned and flaky, about 5 mins per side.
  3. Serve salmon with lemon wedges.
